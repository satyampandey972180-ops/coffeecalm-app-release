# ☕ CoffeeCalm

CoffeeCalm is a safe and supportive platform where students can talk,
connect, and feel heard --- without judgment.

Designed especially for college environments, CoffeeCalm provides
emotional support, meaningful conversations, and topic-based community
spaces.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🚀 Features

-   💬 Instant 1-to-1 Conversations\
-   🕶 Anonymous & Safe Interaction\
-   👥 Topic-Based Group Chats\
-   🤝 Option to Stay Connected After Conversation\
-   📝 Chat History Available\
-   🚫 Block & Safety Controls

## 🎯 Purpose

CoffeeCalm aims to:

-   Promote mental well-being in colleges\
-   Provide immediate peer-based emotional support\
-   Create safe digital spaces for students\
-   Support institutional mental wellness initiatives
